Prosopeople
- Relationship Manager

Prosopeople is a digital auto-prosoporaphy, a technology for managing your relationships.

Keep records about people, with whom you want to build a deeper relationship, by creating dossiers, and writing entries about them. That way, you don't forget what you've done together, what they like, how you feel about them.

I modeled the core features of this app after the user interface of Evernote.com.
